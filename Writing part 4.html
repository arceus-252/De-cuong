🚨 Tình huống (Situation):
You are a member of a travel club. You received an email stating that the upcoming club trip has been cancelled because the travel agency went bankrupt, and the membership fee will be doubled next year to cover the loss.

✉️ Task 1: Write to a friend (Informal)

Write a short email to your friend. Tell your friend your feelings about this and what you plan to do.. Write about 50 words.

Số từ: 0 / ~50

🏛️ Task 2: Write to the President (Formal)

Write an email to the president of the club. Tell them your thoughts about this and what you would like to do. Write 120-150 words.

Số từ: 0 / 120-150
